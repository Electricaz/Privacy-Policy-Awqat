1 Introduction

Welcome to Awqat (the "App"). Awqat is an Islamic prayer times application that helps you stay connected to your daily prayers. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

We built Awqat with your privacy in mind. The App is designed to function with minimal data and does not require any account creation, registration, or login.

2 No Personal Data Collection

Awqat does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no account registration, no login, no social sign-in, and no user profiles.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Browsing or search history
  • Contacts, photos, or any other data from your device

The App operates locally on your device and does not send personal information to any server.

3 Location Data

Awqat requests access to your device's GPS location solely to provide its core features:

  • Prayer time calculations — accurate prayer times based on your geographic coordinates
  • Qibla direction — compass-based direction to the Kaaba in Makkah

Your location data is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to, stored on, or shared with any external server or third party.

You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. If location access is denied, you may manually set your location within the App to continue receiving prayer times.

4 Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind you of prayer times and adhan alerts. These notifications are generated entirely on your device based on your calculated prayer schedule.

No data is sent to external push notification services. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device settings or the App's preferences.

5 Third-Party Services

Awqat does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain ads and does not share any data with advertisers.

If the App uses any third-party libraries or SDKs for functionality (such as mapping or compass features), these operate locally on your device and do not transmit personal data.

6 Data Storage

All App data — including your prayer time preferences, notification settings, and calculation method — is stored locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers.

You can delete all App data at any time by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App from your device.

7 Children's Privacy

Since Awqat does not collect any personal data, the App is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
